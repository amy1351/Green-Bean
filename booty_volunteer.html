About us

We are a nonprofit organization that works to help abused animals. We believe that they are our responsibility and that we all should play our part in helping stop animal cruelty. We take donations that sponsor our efforts to stop puppy mills and hoarders. Our no-kill shelters can provide up to 1000 animals a place to stay, but this is not a long-term solution. This is where you can help. By volunteering, becoming a foster, adopting, or donating, you can join the fight against animal abuse.
